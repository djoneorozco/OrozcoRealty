Our Story

Founded by Juan Josue Orozco, a U.S. military veteran with expertise in data analytics and real estate strategy, OrozcoRealty LLC exists to serve underserved communities and fellow veterans. Based in El Cenizo, Texas, we turn distressed properties into vibrant homes and investments.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, affordable housing to working-class families and veterans by revitalizing neglected homes and transforming them into long-term assets for the community. We specialize in Section 8 housing and rent-to-own models that give families a chance at ownership and security.
