About OrozcoRealty LLC

OrozcoRealty LLC is a veteran-owned real estate investment company based in Texas, dedicated to revitalizing communities and creating opportunities for affordable housing. With a focus on serving fellow veterans and underserved areas like El Cenizo and San Antonio, our mission is grounded in service, integrity, and impact.

We specialize in property acquisition, rehabilitation, and Section 8 rental strategies tailored to meet the needs of families and individuals seeking stable housing solutions. Leveraging veteran loan programs and strategic renovations, we aim to create long-term value in every neighborhood we invest in.
