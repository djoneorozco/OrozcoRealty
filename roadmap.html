OrozcoRealty LLC Development Roadmap

From vision to impact — here's how we’re building a sustainable, veteran-owned real estate brand serving Texas communities with integrity and innovation.

Phase 1: Foundation

  • Veteran Verification Letter (VVL) secured
  • LLC Formation & Branding setup
  • Initial capital via 0% APR credit lines

Phase 2: Property Acquisition

  • Target properties in San Antonio & El Cenizo
  • Begin Section 8 eligibility research
  • Contractor estimates and inspections

Phase 3: Rehab & Renovation

  • Utilize business credit for renovations
  • Quality upgrades for appraised value
  • Prepare for Section 8 voucher inspection

Phase 4: Leasing & Growth

  • 80% LTV refinance based on appraisal
  • Rent to Housing Choice Voucher recipients
  • Repeat investment cycle on next property
